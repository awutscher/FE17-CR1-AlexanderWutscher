Recipe of the Month

Spaghetti Bolognese

Shopping List for 4 People

  • 350g Ground Beef
  • 370g Spaghetti
  • 1 Can Tomatoes
  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Gloves Garlic
  • 1 Glass Red Wine
  • 2 Tablespoons Oregano

Preparation

For the spaghetti bolognese, first peel and finely chop the onion and garlic cloves. Remove the ends of the carrot, peel and finely grate the carrot. Wash the parsley, shake dry and chop very finely.

Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water according to the packet instructions until al dente, strain and you're done.

Heat the olive oil in a saucepan and sauté the onion and garlic for 4 minutes. Then add the grated carrot and sauté briefly. Then add the minced meat and sauté briefly. Add the tomato passata, red wine and oregano and allow to reduce slightly. With the lid closed, simmer gently over a low heat for about 40 minutes. Stir in the tomato paste and season with parsley, salt and pepper.

Arrange the noodles on the plates, pour the sauce over them and garnish with a few fresh basil leaves.

Tip: Do not quench the spaghetti - this way the pasta starch is not washed off and the sauce sticks better.
